Currently, the following subdomains are active:

  • md.hecke.rs, a collaborative markdown editor and host.
  • z.hecke.rs, a Zulip instance. For various reasons (lack of use and annoying upkeep), z.hecke.rs is no longer in service for the forseeable future.
  • mail.hecke.rs, a mail server.
  • rss.hecke.rs, a tt-rss installation.

Some of these services are open-registration. Others are closed-registration for various reasons; email due to spam, tt-rss due to it being kinda insecure for multi-user, etc. If you want an account on a closed service, just email the hecke.rs admin, Erin Moon <erin@hecke.rs>.
